Chiku and Piku

These are my parrots.

Chiku and Piku. I have brought them from Patna. They are very playful, loyal, and talkative. They can also learn tricks and show off its acrobat skills.

My parrots are bright, gentle, independent, medium-sized. They are hardy and relatively quiet. They are favorite among fanciers and is becoming more popular among pet lovers.

The parrots I have are found in East India to Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh in the South, Sri Lanka.

Piku and Chiku have a cage tall enough to accommodate their long tail feathers. Dad says that thier tail can be up to 14 inches in length. Both are chewing machines and need lots of softwood toys to keep them happy.

I have given them a few rope toys and a couple of indestructible acrylic toys. Puzzle toys that hide nuts or dried fruit can be especially entertaining for them. They are always ready for a challenge.
